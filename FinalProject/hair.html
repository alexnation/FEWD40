The Basics

So, girlboss, this blog is about SYSTEMS. And no matter what kind of hair you have, all the product and all the tools in the world can't help you if you don't have a good haircut from an expert stylist. Your first task is to find the best hairstylist that you can afford - find someone with fantastic hair and get their stylist's number. Think about how much work you're willing to put in, and be realistic. For example, I get my hair cut once or twice a year and have super low maintenance balyage that requires no upkeep at all. This is for two reasons: 1. I can't afford $100+ for a haircut that often. 2. Ditto color - balyage can be $180 and up.
